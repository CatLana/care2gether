Care2gether — Caring for nature, together.

A modern platform empowering communities to protect, restore, and celebrate their natural spaces.

The Problem

Our communities are losing natural spaces due to pollution, neglect, and lack of coordinated action.

  • Loss of green areas
  • Damage to local ecosystems
  • Decreased biodiversity
  • Unsafe, neglected public spaces

People want to help, but most don’t know where to start.

The Gap

There is no simple, community-driven platform that helps people:

  • Join environmental cleanups and restoration events
  • Learn about local environmental issues
  • Make consistent, meaningful impact
  • Connect with like‑minded volunteers

The Solution: Care2gether

Care2gether brings volunteers, neighbours, and organisers together to restore and protect natural spaces—one community at a time.

It empowers people not only to act, but to learn, connect, and lead change.

Create Campaigns

Organisers can easily publish local volunteering initiatives with photos, details, and scheduled events.

Discover Activities

Volunteers can explore nearby environmental events based on location, time, and interests.

Connect & Collaborate

Meet people who care about the same mission—share progress, celebrate wins, and build community impact.

Track Your Impact

Earn badges, track volunteering hours, and see how your contributions help your community thrive.
